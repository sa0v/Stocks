# Stocks

This notebook analyzes the closing price of the stock of Apple Inc. over a period of five years from the present. The data used in this notebook is provided by **Yahoo! Finance** via the **yfinance** module. The intention is to demonstrate the effectiveness of "ensemble" modelling techniques in predicting stock closing prices, using the stock of Apple Inc. (stock symbol AAPL) as an example. These techniques may potentially be applicable to the analysis of stock closing prices for **any** company or stock symbol.
